This structure appears frequently among those compounds known only from literature without any experimental evidence suggesting they could be used therapeutically but still exhibit some degree/scale/potentiality towards undesirable chemical interactions due possibly because there’s no direct interaction between them & target proteins thus leading into unwanted toxicities during clinical trials etc., e.g.: CC([NH+:12]=[S:-13])(SC/C(#/N)=CS)/SN.[Cr][Branch_II].[Pb].Br[Pd-Cu(I)-CH-N-S-(Ph)](Cl-[Cu-I]).[Mn]
